📢 D365 Posts Bot

by Mark Carrington

Teams notifications of D365 Posts

Users are notified immediately when a new post is written on a record they're interested in. No configuration required - the bot works out who should be notified for each post automatically.

Reply directly from Teams

No jumping around between different apps, just reply directly to a notification within Teams. The reply is saved back to D365 and notifications are sent out as necessary.

Free!

Yes, really! Either use this hosted version or set up your own with the open-source code.
